Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Eternal Yog("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it. By using this website or contacting us via WhatsApp, you agree to the terms described here.

Information We Collect

We do not operate any registration forms, databases, or user accounts on this website. The only personal information we receive is what you voluntarily share when you reach out to us directly:

  • Your name and phone number, shared when you initiate a WhatsApp conversation with us
  • Any details you choose to include in your enquiry message (e.g. preferred class, health conditions)

How We Use Your Information

Information you share with us is used solely to respond to your class enquiry and to confirm your booking. We do not use your information for marketing without your consent, and we do not build profiles or track your behaviour across sessions.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. WhatsApp conversations are subject to Meta's own privacy policy, which governs how that platform handles message data.

Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics trackers, or any third-party tracking scripts. Standard browser behaviour (such as caching of page assets) applies but is outside our control.

Data Retention

WhatsApp conversations are retained within the WhatsApp application until deleted by either party. We do not store your personal information in any separate system or database.

Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of any personal data you have shared with us. To do so, please contact us directly via WhatsApp and we will action your request promptly.

Contact

For any privacy-related queries, please reach out via WhatsApp. Contact details are available on the home page.
