ABOUT US

Eternal Yog is a space where ancient wisdom meets modern living. Founded with a deep reverence for the authentic traditions of yogsadhna, we guide seekers on a journey inward — toward stillness, clarity, and the remembrance of who they truly are. Our practice is rooted in the classical teachings of Hatha, Raja, and Bhakti yoga, woven with the timeless knowledge of Ayurveda and Sanskrit philosophy. Whether you are taking your first step or deepening an existing practice, Eternal Yog meets you exactly where you are.

WHY JOIN US

This is not the class you attend...It's the space you return to- for balance, clarity and stillness

The journey with the guide

Every practice is a conversation between the body, the breath, and the self. As your guide, I do not teach yoga — I walk alongside you as you remember it. Trained in the classical lineages of Hatha and Raja yoga at the foothills of the Himalayas, I have spent years studying not just the postures, but the philosophy, the silence, and the rhythm that underlies all authentic practice. What I offer is not a curriculum — it is a living transmission. Each session is shaped by where you are, not where you think you should be.

Yoga is not just practiced on the mat... It is a way of life we carry beyond it.
